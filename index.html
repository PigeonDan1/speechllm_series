Reproducible Evaluation Pipeline

Unified post-processing, metrics, and agent-based verification.

What we standardize

  • Answer normalization before any task-specific metric computation.
  • Dataset-aligned metric definitions and direction-aware scoring.
  • Format checks for structured outputs and parser compatibility.
  • Failure reporting with explicit pass/fail and missing-output traces.
  • Versioned configs for prompts, routing, and evaluation settings.
